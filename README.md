# Walmart Retail Sales Analytics

## Project Overview

Retail organizations generate large volumes of transactional data that can provide valuable insights into customer behavior, product performance, and revenue generation.

This project analyzes Walmart sales transactions to uncover trends in product demand, customer purchasing behavior, branch performance, and revenue generation. The objective is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses need to answer important operational questions:

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which branches perform best?
* How do customer purchasing patterns vary?
* Which payment methods are preferred?
* How does revenue change over time?

This project uses exploratory data analysis and business intelligence techniques to identify key performance drivers within Walmart sales data.

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains transactional sales records including:

### Customer Information

* Gender
* Customer Type

### Product Information

* Product Line
* Unit Price
* Quantity

### Sales Information

* Total Sales
* Tax (VAT)
* Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)

### Store Information

* Branch
* City

### Payment Information

* Cash
* Credit Card
* E-wallet

---

## Key Business Questions Answered

### Which payment method is most commonly used?

Customer payment preferences were analyzed to understand transaction behavior.

### Which product line generates the highest sales volume?

Product categories were compared based on total quantity sold.

### Which product line generates the highest revenue?

Revenue contribution was evaluated across all product categories.

### Which city generates the highest revenue?

Sales performance was compared across store locations.

### Which month produced the highest revenue?

Monthly sales trends were analyzed to identify seasonal patterns.

### Which branches outperform average sales performance?

Branch-level benchmarking was performed using sales volume metrics.

---

## Key Insights

### Revenue Drivers

* Certain product categories contribute disproportionately to overall revenue.
* Revenue concentration highlights opportunities for inventory optimization.

### Customer Purchasing Behavior

* Payment method preferences reveal customer transaction habits.
* Product demand varies across customer segments.

### Branch Performance

* Sales performance differs across store locations.
* High-performing branches can provide best practices for operational improvements.

### Sales Trends

* Monthly revenue analysis identifies periods of stronger business activity.
* Seasonal trends can support forecasting and inventory planning.
